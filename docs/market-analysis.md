- **TAM:** Large and growing
- **Customers:** Enterprises deploying ML/AI models
- **Pain Points:** 
  - Prompt injection attacks
  - Model poisoning
  - Data leakage
  - Performance bottlenecks

#### 2. Agentic AI Security (Emerging)
- **TAM:** $3.5B by 2028
- **Customers:** AI-native companies, autonomous systems
- **Pain Points:**
  - Multi-agent coordination security
  - Autonomous decision validation
  - Agent action monitoring

## Customer Willingness to Pay

### WTP Calculation Methodology
```
Market Minimum WTP = $1,000 + ($500 × Feature Level)
Market Potential WTP = $2,000 + ($1,500 × Feature Level)
Optimal Price Point = 70-80% of Market Potential WTP
```

### Key Insights
- Enterprise customers willing to pay **2-3x more** for agentic AI security
- Professional tier positioned at optimal price-value point
- Free tier creates value gap that drives conversion

## Market Trends

### Key Trends Driving Adoption

1. **AI Governance Regulations**
   - EU AI Act, US AI Executive Order
   - Compliance requirements driving security investment

2. **High-Profile AI Security Incidents**
   - Prompt injection attacks making headlines
   - Increasing awareness of AI vulnerabilities

3. **Enterprise AI Adoption**
   - 80% of enterprises planning AI deployments
   - Security cited as #1 concern

4. **Agentic AI Emergence**
   - Autonomous agents becoming mainstream
   - New security challenges requiring new solutions

5. **Performance Requirements**
   - Real-time AI applications demanding low latency
   - Security cannot compromise performance

## Market Risks

### Potential Challenges

1. **Market Education**
   - AI security still emerging category
   - Need to educate market on threats and solutions

2. **Competition**
   - Large players (Palo Alto, Cisco) entering space
   - Need to establish differentiation quickly

3. **Technology Evolution**
   - Rapid AI advancement may outpace security solutions
   - Need continuous innovation

4. **Economic Factors**
   - Budget constraints may slow enterprise AI adoption
   - Need to demonstrate clear ROI
